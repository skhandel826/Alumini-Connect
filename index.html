About Us

Our history and achievements

Connecting Alumni Since 2015

Established in 2025, our alumni network aims to unite graduates through a shared space for networking, mentorship, and ongoing engagement with the university.

Our network facilitates mentorship opportunities, career development, and lifelong learning for all members. We organize regular events and reunions to strengthen these connections.
